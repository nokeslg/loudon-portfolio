01 — Background

I design things
that work.

I'm Loudon Nokes — a UI/UX and graphic designer studying Interaction Design at the University of Kansas. I work at the intersection of visual clarity and human behavior, building interfaces that communicate precisely and feel inevitable.

Before design, I spent years as a behavioral interventionist — working with people under pressure taught me how humans actually process information, make decisions, and break down. That background informs everything I build.

I move fast, design with intent, and care deeply about the craft of both screen and print.
